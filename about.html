about

TIC TAC TOE is a game for one(you can play with yourself) or two players who take(s) turn marking the spaces in a three-by-three grid with X OR O. the player who succeeds in placing three of their marks in a horizontal, vertical or diagonal row is the winner. A result of draw is expected if both players play with similar tactics.

maximum Players 2
minimum Players 1
skills required tactics, common sense
Alphabets type X or O
